How to Master the Library Printer

The KCC library printer is extremely useful to all; staff and students alike. It can perform many tasks that the majority don't know. This guide will hopefully assist with all things this excellent printer can do.

Challenges tackled in this guide:

  • Modification of document eg. size, scaling, back to back

  • Photocopying and scanning

  • How to load with paper

  • How to fix jammed paper

  • Printer issues eg. printer not responding

  • Changing Image quality
